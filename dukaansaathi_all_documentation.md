# DukaanSaathi

## Project Description
Create comprehensive product documentation for a mobile + web application called **“Dukaan Bright”**.

## 🧠 Product Overview

Dukaan Bright is an AI-powered inventory and profit optimization app designed for small and medium shop owners (kirana stores, local vendors). It helps users manage inventory, track product expiration dates, and automatically optimize selling prices to maximize profit.

The app is built specifically for users with limited technical knowledge (10th–12th grade education), so the entire experience must be simple, visual, and easy to use without training.

---

## 🎯 Core Problem

Small shop owners currently:

* Do not track inventory efficiently
* Lose money due to expired products
* Set prices based on guesswork
* Do not account for fixed expenses (rent, electricity, salaries)

---

## 💡 Solution

Dukaan Bright solves this by:

* Providing a simple inventory management system
* Tracking expiry dates with alerts
* Taking user input for monthly expenses (liabilities)
* Using AI to suggest optimal selling prices based on:

  * Cost price (CP)
  * Expenses
  * Demand trends
* Highlighting best-performing and slow-moving products

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📦 Inventory Management

* Add/edit products (name, quantity, CP, SP, expiry date)
* Color-coded alerts:

  * Red → expiring soon
  * Yellow → low stock
  * Green → healthy stock

---

### ⏰ Expiry Tracking

* Automatic alerts for near-expiry products
* Prevents losses from unsold goods

---

### 💸 Expense-Based Profit Calculation

* Users input:

  * Rent
  * Electricity
  * Salaries
  * Other costs
* App calculates actual profitability based on these inputs

---

### 🤖 AI Pricing & Insights

* Identifies:

  * Best-performing products
  * Slow-moving inventory
* Suggests:

  * Price increases/decreases
  * Stock adjustments
* Provides simple, actionable recommendations like:

  * “Increase price by ₹5”
  * “Reduce stock of this item”

---

### 📊 Dashboard

* Daily and monthly profit overview
* Alerts for stock and expiry
* Quick insights for decision-making

---

### 🖥️ Web Dashboard (Advanced Usage)

* Inventory table with bulk editing
* Search, filter, and sorting
* AI insights with comparison views
* Faster data management for power users
